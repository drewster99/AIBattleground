**App Name:** AI Arena

**Narrative:**

"AI Arena" perfectly encapsulates your application's core functionality of pitting multiple AI language models against each other in a competitive yet informative setting. The name is concise and memorable, appealing to both technical and non-technical users by invoking the excitement of an arena where performances are showcased side by side. It conveys a sense of engagement and fun, while also highlighting the serious analytical capabilities of the tool. "AI Arena" will stand out in the Mac App Store, making it easy for users to find and remember, and its dynamic imagery lends itself well to impactful marketing strategies.
