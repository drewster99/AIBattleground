Top 3 App Name Choices:

1. AI Fusion
2. AI Nexus
3. AI Synthesis

After considering the technical and non-technical resonance, conveyance of intended meanings, screen appearance, ease of finding in the app store, and potential for marketing, my very best choice for the app name is "AI Fusion." This name conveys the idea of bringing multiple AI models together in a unified interface, creating a sense of connectedness and integration. It is easy to remember, descriptive, and has a modern and sleek appeal that would resonate well with both technical and non-technical users. This name also opens up opportunities for creative marketing strategies, emphasizing the fusion of various AI services in one powerful platform.
